A little about me.

I'm a Data Engineer based in Mumbai with 3+ years of experience across product companies and enterprise consulting. My core stack is SQL, Python, PySpark, Databricks, and AWS — and I've delivered work spanning payment reconciliation, AI-driven analytics, and large-scale data migrations.

Currently at BookMyShow, where I've contributed to a full Redshift-to-Databricks warehouse decommissioning, authored a reusable migration framework that other engineering teams adopted, and built end-to-end systems for payment reconciliation and CX agent-quality analytics using Databricks AI Functions.

Previously at Tredence Analytics, I worked on enterprise data platform builds for global consumer-goods, retail, and FMCG clients — designing dimensional models, building PySpark pipelines, and driving query optimization for large-scale analytical workloads.

I'm particularly interested in problems where data engineering meets AI — using LLMs to score, classify, and enrich data in production pipelines. I'm open to remote roles across product companies, startups, and modern data teams.
